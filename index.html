Frequently Asked

Honest answers, before you commit.

The questions our pilot customers ask most often.

Is the AI repair estimate really free?+

Yes — completely free, no signup or credit card. Snap a photo, answer a few guided questions, and we'll show you a price range plus nearby shops.

What happens to my photo?+

Your photo is sent to our AI for diagnosis only and is not stored on our servers or shared with shops unless you choose to book a repair.

What if my device can't be repaired?+

If a partner shop determines the repair isn't worth it (cost > replacement, unsafe, or parts unavailable), you'll get an honest 'don't repair' recommendation and pay nothing for the diagnosis.

Do repairs come with a warranty?+

Every FixLoop partner shop offers a minimum 60-day warranty on parts and labor. Many shops offer 90 or 120 days — you'll see each shop's warranty before you book.

When do I pay, and to whom?+

You pay the repair shop directly after the work is complete and you're satisfied. FixLoop never holds your payment.

Where is FixLoop available?+

We're launching our pilot in Vancouver, BC. Want us in your city? Drop your email below and we'll let you know when we expand.
